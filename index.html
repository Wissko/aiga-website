FAQ

Common Questions,
Straight Answers.

How long does setup actually take?

▼
The Starter package is live in 5 business days. Growth takes 2 weeks. Pro AI is a 6-week full rollout. We need roughly 2 hours of your time total — a 30-min onboarding call and a 30-min review at the end. We handle everything else.

What if I already have a website?

▼
No problem. We can audit your existing site and recommend improvements, or we can build you a new one. Often, a fresh build on modern infrastructure outperforms patching an older site — but we'll be honest about what's actually worth doing.

Do I need technical knowledge?

▼
None at all. That's precisely why we exist. You run your business — we handle the digital infrastructure end-to-end. Our clients are business owners, not tech teams.

What's the real ROI?

▼
The Starter at $490 breaks even with 20 new customers — less than 1 per week. GBP optimisation alone typically drives a 70% increase in profile visits. Our clients see payback within the first fortnight in most cases.

Is the AI voice agent available in Australia?

▼
Yes. The Vapi.ai voice agent works with Australian phone numbers and can be configured with an Australian accent. It handles inbound calls 24/7, logs bookings to Google Sheets, and transfers to a human when needed.

What happens after setup — ongoing support?

▼
Growth includes 60-day support. Pro AI includes ongoing monthly reporting, campaigns, and a dedicated account manager. For Starter clients, we offer optional monthly retainers for continued optimisation.
